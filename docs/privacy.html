Privacy Policy

Cuttle Export Helper — Last updated May 2026

Cuttle Export Helper is a browser extension that helps you batch-export designs from the Cuttle editor. This policy explains what data the extension does and does not collect.

Data we collect

None. Cuttle Export Helper does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

Data stored on your device

The extension saves your last-used settings locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage. This includes:

  • Parameter names and values you've entered
  • Your filename template
  • Your chosen export format and batch mode

This data never leaves your browser. It is only used to restore your settings the next time you open the extension.

Permissions

The extension requests the following browser permissions:

  • Active tab — to identify the Cuttle editor tab
  • Scripting — to trigger exports within the Cuttle editor page
  • Storage — to save your settings locally between sessions

These permissions are used solely to perform exports on cuttle.xyz. The extension does not access any other websites or browser data.

Third parties

This extension does not use analytics, advertising, or any third-party services.
